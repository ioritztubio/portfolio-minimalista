Oris Dental Scan

AI-powered dental pre-diagnosis tool for mobile users.

Overview

Users upload a photograph and receive a preliminary oral health assessment in minutes, generated by a custom AI pipeline. The product is live and used by real patients.

The project was built at Skootik within the PKF Attest AI Department and funded by the European Union, the Spanish Government and the Basque Government.

My contribution

  • Built frontend flows with Next.js and TypeScript.
  • Contributed to backend services with Django and Python.
  • Worked across the product stack on a production application.

Technology

Next.js · TypeScript · Django · Python · PostgreSQL · AWS · AI
